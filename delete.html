Privacy Policy for Beer Finder

Effective Date: October 14, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Beer Finder collects the following information:

  • Location Data: We access your device's location to show nearby beer venues and calculate distances.
  • Photos: When you choose to upload venue photos, we access your camera or photo gallery.
  • Account Information: Name and email when you sign up via Google Sign-In.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Display nearby beer venues based on your location
  • Allow you to upload and share venue photos
  • Manage your account and saved preferences

3. Permissions

Camera Permission (android.permission.CAMERA): Used only when you choose to take photos of venues. We do not access your camera without your explicit action.

Location Permission: Used to find venues near you. You can deny location access, but the app's core features require it.

4. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) and protected according to industry standards.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required to provide app functionality (e.g., Firebase/Google services).

6. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.

7. Contact Us

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: info@beerfinderapp.com
